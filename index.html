Flying cat

It is often seen that cats fall from a height of more than 20 meters and are still safe. In fact, this is because they have "righting reflex". The cat's eyes and inner ear balance organs will tell the cat where it is in the air, so cats can land with their feet accurately. Even if the cat has no tail, it also has this ability. However, the excrement shoveling officers should not ignore the cat because of this. They should take protective measures for the cat at home and try their best not to hurt the cat.
